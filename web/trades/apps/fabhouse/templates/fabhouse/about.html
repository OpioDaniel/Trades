Lee van der Walt is an Interior designer who has been operating as a designer and business owner for almost 20 years all over Africa, the Middle East and Europe.

Décor Beautiful (Lee’s first interior design company) came into existence in Johannesburg South Africa in 2000 as a full fledged design studio with a curtain and furniture manufacturing division. Décor Beautiful expanded into Uganda in 2008 as Décor Design and has been operating in East, West and South Africa for the last 11 years.

Fab House was created in 2017 in Uganda with the sole purpose of supplying the Architectural and Interior design industry with quality creative products. Fab House is also in the process of expanding these products and services to the whole of East Africa.

Working internationally with various design finishes – granted us the opportunity to supply East Africa with the same quality interior finishes that is world-wide available to all first world industries.

Fab House obtained the sole rights for the distribution of all Sibu products in East Africa. We are in the process of obtaining the rights for Southern and West Africa.
